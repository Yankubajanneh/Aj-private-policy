Privacy Policy for AJ

Effective Date: May 8, 2026

Welcome to AJ. Your privacy, trust, and emotional safety matter deeply to us.

AJ is an emotionally intelligent AI assistant designed to support conversation, emotional reflection, self-awareness, productivity, learning, and personal growth. Because users may share sensitive thoughts, emotions, and personal experiences while using AJ, we are committed to handling user data responsibly, transparently, and securely.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide Directly

  • Messages and conversations
  • Profile information
  • Account details
  • Uploaded images or files
  • Feedback and preferences
  • Goals and communication settings

B. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • Language settings
  • Usage analytics
  • Crash reports
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Approximate location based on IP address

C. AI Conversation Data

Conversations with AJ may be temporarily processed and stored to improve response quality, maintain optional memory features, improve safety systems, and enhance emotional support features.

We do NOT sell private conversations to advertisers.

2. Emotional Support Disclaimer

AJ is NOT:

  • A licensed therapist
  • A medical professional
  • A psychiatrist
  • A crisis service
  • A replacement for professional mental health care

AJ provides emotional support, reflective conversation, educational insights, grounding suggestions, and wellness-oriented discussion.

If you are experiencing severe emotional distress, self-harm thoughts, abuse danger, or a mental health emergency, contact a qualified professional or local emergency services immediately.

3. How We Use Information

  • Operate and maintain AJ
  • Improve AI conversations
  • Personalize user experiences
  • Maintain optional memory features
  • Improve safety systems
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Improve app performance
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Memory Features

AJ may offer optional memory features that allow the assistant to remember:

  • User preferences
  • Goals
  • Communication styles
  • Recurring topics
  • Ongoing conversations

Users may disable memory, delete memories, or request removal of stored information.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user information.

  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Secure cloud infrastructure
  • Restricted internal access
  • Authentication systems
  • Security monitoring

However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

6. Sharing of Information

We do NOT sell personal user data.

Limited information may be shared only when necessary for:

  • Trusted infrastructure providers
  • Analytics and app functionality
  • Legal compliance
  • Safety investigations
  • Preventing abuse or illegal activity

7. AI Limitations

AJ uses artificial intelligence systems that may occasionally generate inaccurate, incomplete, or imperfect responses.

Users should not rely on AJ for:

  • Medical diagnoses
  • Emergency decisions
  • Legal advice
  • Financial advice
  • Crisis intervention

8. Children's Privacy

AJ is not intended for children under the minimum age required by local laws.

9. User Rights & Controls

Depending on your region, users may have rights to:

  • Access personal information
  • Request deletion
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent
  • Export data

10. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary to provide services, improve systems, comply with legal obligations, and enforce policies.

11. International Data Processing

Depending on infrastructure providers and user location, information may be processed in different countries.

12. Third-Party Services

AJ may integrate with third-party services such as cloud hosting, analytics, authentication systems, payment processors, and AI model providers.

13. Advertising

If advertising is introduced in the future, advertisements will remain separate from AI conversations and advertisers will not receive private conversation content.

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to improve clarity, comply with legal requirements, or reflect platform updates.

15. Contact Information

Users may contact us through the support methods provided inside AJ.

16. Ethical AI Commitment

AJ is designed to support users responsibly while avoiding manipulative, emotionally harmful, or dependency-based AI behavior.

AJ is intended to support human growth — not replace real human relationships, professional care, or real-world support systems.

17. Consent

By using AJ, you acknowledge that you understand this Privacy Policy, understand the limitations of AI systems, and consent to the processing of information as described here.
